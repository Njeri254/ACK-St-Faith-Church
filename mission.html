St. Faith's Mission Team

As a dedicated team, the Mission Team is driven by the need to see that they follow in Christ's footsteps as instructed in (Luke 6:27-30). The team's goal is to reach the lost, the needy and the vulnerable in the Society to share the Gospel of Christ in the whole world as instructed by Jesu (Matt 28:19-20) thereby, promoting unity and well-being of the Society at large.
